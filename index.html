Feeling tired?

We know classes are stressful, and 8 hours of sleep can be hard to come by. That's why we've curated a list of superior napping spots at Dartmouth College, chosen for their provision of shade, peace and quiet, and comfy furniture.

Our top picks for the month of April

The red couches located on the first floor of the Visual Arts building are plush and inviting. There will be some students milling around, but they're typically rather quiet.

The grass on the Green just outside Baker-Berry Library is a nice place to settle down on a warm, sunny day! Sadly, due to the subzero temperatures that Hanover is prone to providing in the winter, this is a seasonally limited option.
